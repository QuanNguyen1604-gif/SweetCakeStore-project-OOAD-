LOYAL CUSTOMERS PROGRAM

As a heartfelt appreciation to our valued customers for choosing Matie Cake for their sweet celebrations, we are pleased to offer exclusive benefits specially designed for our loyal members.

Discounts are applicable to products sold at regular prices only.

In case multiple promotions are available, the promotion with the highest value will be applied.

Loyalty members will receive priority notifications regarding promotions and newly launched products from Matie Cake.

Members are eligible to participate in exclusive promotions reserved specifically for VIP customers.

The program is valid at all Matie Cake stores as well as our online store.

Promotions applied through the Grab application cannot be combined with this program.

Matie Cake reserves the right to update or modify promotion terms in order to better suit customer needs and actual operating conditions.

Delivery promotions are applicable for orders with a minimum value of 250,000 VND.
